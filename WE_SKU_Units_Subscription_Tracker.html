Data persists per-user via Claude memory storage. CapEx Revenue = list price × units sold, applied where the SKU is a hardware/kinetic asset. OpEx Revenue = list price × active subscriptions, applied where the SKU is licensed software. Hybrid SKUs post to both; Sovereign Catalog entries are unclassified pending product-type data. Gross Profit = (CapEx + OpEx Revenue) × Margin %, where Margin % is benchmarked per product type to the nearest real-world FY2025/FY2026 comparable's disclosed gross margin (Cisco ~65%, Palo Alto Networks 77–80% product / 72–73% subscription, NVIDIA 72–75%, ServiceNow 78–82%, Dell/HPE server 18–28%, Tesla automotive ~17% / energy storage ~29%, Anduril's targeted 40–45% vs. legacy defense primes' 7–12%, and blended early-stage quantum-hardware economics ~35–45%) — not fabricated figures. Sovereign Catalog SKUs carry no list price and are excluded from margin/profit math.
